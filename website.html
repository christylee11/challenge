INTRODUCTION

Hi, Tiffany! The format of this website was based off of a template from w3schools. The weather itself was taken from OpenWeatherMap, using their APIs (specifically the "Current weather data" API). The little icons used were from Font Awesome, and the stylistic classes used throughout are referenced here, at w3schools. Based on pressing time constraints (many, many assignments this week!) and the amount of previous experience I have in web development (that would be none, actually. But don't worry- I'm taking two COS classes next semester which focus heavily on front-end development, so I'll be a seasoned pro by summer!), I was only able to play around with changing some fonts, colors, icons, and of course, content on the website. I did still manage to learn quite a bit in the process of doing this, though, so I hope you enjoy!
